Southfield, MI Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Southfield is a city in southeastern Oakland County in the U.S. state of Michigan. An inner-ring suburb of Detroit, Southfield borders Detroit to the north, lying roughly 15 miles (24.1 km) northwest of downtown. As of the 2020 census, the city had a population of 76,618.
