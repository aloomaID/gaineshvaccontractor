Avimor, ID Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Avimor is a census-designated place (CDP) in Ada County, Idaho, United States. It is on the northern edge of the county, bordered to the northeast by Boise County and to the west by Idaho State Highway 55. The Spring Valley is in the western part of the CDP. The community is 8 miles (13 km) northeast of Eagle and 15 miles (24 km) north of Boise, the state capital.
