Francisville, KY Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Francisville is a census-designated place (CDP) in Boone County, Kentucky, United States, and a wealthy suburb of Greater Cincinnati. Its population was 7,944 as of the 2010 census. Francisville shares ZIP code 41048 with the neighboring CDP of Hebron. It is the northernmost community in the state.
