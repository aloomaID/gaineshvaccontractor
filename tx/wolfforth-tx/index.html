Wolfforth, TX Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Wolfforth (/ˈwʊlfɔːrθ/ WUUL-forth) is a town located in Lubbock County, Texas, United States. The town was named for two brothers, George Wolffarth, who was a rancher in the area, and Eastin Wolffarth, who was Lubbock County Sheriff circa 1900. The population was 3,670 at the 2010 census.
