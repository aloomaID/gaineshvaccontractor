Carmel Valley Village, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Carmel Valley Village is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) in Monterey County, California, United States. In 1946, Byington Ford and Tirey L. Ford Jr. developed the Carmel Valley Village, which included an airpark, shops, and homes.: p4  At the time of the 2020 census the CDP population was 4,524, up from 4,407 at the 2010 census.
