Casper, WY Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Casper is a city in, and the county seat of, Natrona County, Wyoming, United States. Casper is the second-most populous city in the state, with the population at 59,038 as of the 2020 census. Casper is nicknamed 'The Oil City' and has a long history of oil boomtown and cowboy culture, dating back to the development of the nearby Salt Creek Oil Field.
