New Rochelle, NY Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

New Rochelle (/ˌnuːrəˈʃɛl/ NEW-Rə-SHEL; older French: La Nouvelle-Rochelle) is a city in Westchester County, New York, United States, in the southeastern portion of the state.
